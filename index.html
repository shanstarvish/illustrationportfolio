Shannon Starvish

Illustration Portfolio

Soft lines. Honest stories. Illustration for graphic novels, editorial, and comics.

Hi, I’m Shannon—an illustrator drawn to storytelling that feels honest, thoughtful, and a little imperfect in all the right ways. My work blends natural colors with soft, organic linework—like flipping through a well-loved sketchbook at the end of the day. I create illustrations for graphic novels, editorial features, and comics, focusing on character, mood, and subtle details that invite readers to linger. Instead of heavy outlines, I use pencil-like textures and hand-drawn lines that capture the quiet, expressive moments that bring stories to life. Whether you’re crafting a graphic novel, an editorial story, or a personal project, I’m here to help translate your ideas into visuals that feel intimate, human, and thoughtfully made.
